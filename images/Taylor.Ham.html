Habbersett Scrapple & Taylor Pork Roll Combo




From
Habbersett Scrapple

(Customer Reviews)










$69



+ Free Shipping



























Get it this Tuesday if you order in the next 17 hours,
or specify your delivery date (up to 90 days) at checkout.













Founded by Isaac S. Habbersett and passed through many family generations, Habbersett has been a household name for over 150 years. Their commitment to using the highest quality pork, all natural ingredients and unique blend of spices is why theyre known as simply the best scrapple around. Pan-fried to perfection, this Dutch-originated delicacy is the ultimate crispy, salty bite you want with your breakfast eggs and toast. Taylor Pork Roll is also a Philly favorite. It is made from pork, spices and other flavorsall kept secret by the manufacturers of this popular meat.








More Details












    

  • This package includes 2 packs of Habbersett Original Scrapple + 2 packs of Taylor Pork Roll (pre-sliced)
    • 

  • Each pack weighs 1 lb. and serves 3
    • 

  • Each pack of Taylor Pork Roll includes 8 slices
    • 











Ingredients












Scrapple: Pork stock, pork scraps, cornmeal, wheat flour, salt and spices










Instructions / Storage












    

  • Meats are shipped frozen with ice packs which may thaw partially or fully during transit
    • 

  • Upon arrival, place meats in the refrigerator or freezer to serve later
    • 

  • To serve, allow meats to thaw in the fridge overnight. Slice into desired thickness and pan fry until browned and crispy
    • 

  • Meats can be stored in the refrigerator for 7 days or in the freezer for up to 1 year
    •
